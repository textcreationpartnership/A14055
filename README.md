##Content Summary##

#####Front#####
A nevv booke of ſpirituall Phyſik for dyuerſe diſeaſes of the nobilitie and gentlemen of Englande, m
1. ¶To the ryght honorable Dukes & Erles, the Duke of Northfolke, the Duke of Suthfolke, the Erle of Arundale, the Erle of Derbi, the Erle of Shroſbery, the Erle of Huntyngton, the Erle of Combrelande, the Erle of Weſtmerlande, the Erle of Penbrook, and the Erle of Warwik William Turner Phyſician wyſſheth perfyt knowlege in Goddes holy worde and grace to lyue acordyng vnto the ſame.

1. ¶Yf thou be mynded, (gētle reder) to rede thys booke here folowynge wyth pleaſure and profyt: amende theſe fautes folowynge as I ſhall teache thee.

1. The contentes of thys booke.

#####Body#####

1. To the noble men and gentle men of Englande.

1. ¶Of the office, workes, learnynge and knowlege that belonge vnto a noble or gentilman.

1. ¶Of the hole Palſey or numnes thorowe all the bodye.

1. ¶ Of the Dropſye.

1. 3 Of the Romyſhe Poxe.

1. 4. Of the Leprye.

1. ¶The prayer of ye prophete Daniel conteyned in the .ix. chaptre of hys boke, very neceſſary for thys troubleſome tyme.

#####Back#####
¶ Imprented at Rome by the vaticane churche, by Marcus Antonius Conſtantius.Otherwyſe called, thraſo
